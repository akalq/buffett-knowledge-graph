2023年度报告

查理·芒格——伯克希尔·哈撒韦的建筑师

English
Charlie Munger died on November 28, just 33 days before his 100th birthday. Though born and raised in Omaha, he spent 80% of his life domiciled elsewhere. Consequently, it was not until 1959 when he was 35 that I first met him. In 1962, he decided that he should take up money management. Three years later he told me – correctly! – that I had made a dumb decision in buying control of Berkshire. But, he assured me, since I had already made the move, he would tell me how to correct my mistake.
中文翻译
查理·芒格于11月28日去世，距离他100岁生日只有33天。虽然他在奥马哈出生和长大，但他人生中80%的时间住在其他地方。因此，直到1959年他35岁时，我才第一次见到他。1962年，他决定从事资金管理。三年后，他告诉我——正确的！——我收购伯克希尔的控制权是一个愚蠢的决定。但是，他向我保证，既然我已经采取了行动，他会告诉我如何纠正我的错误。
English
In what I next relate, bear in mind that Charlie and his family did not have a dime invested in the small investing partnership that I was then managing and whose money I had used for the Berkshire purchase. Moreover, neither of us expected that Charlie would ever own a share of Berkshire stock. Nevertheless, Charlie, in 1965, promptly advised me: "Warren, forget about ever buying another company like Berkshire. But now that you control Berkshire, add to it wonderful businesses purchased at fair prices and give up buying fair businesses at wonderful prices. In other words, abandon everything you learned from your hero, Ben Graham. It works but only when practiced at small scale." With much back-sliding I subsequently followed his instructions.
中文翻译
在我接下来讲述的事情中，请记住，查理和他的家人没有一分钱投资于我当时管理的小型投资合伙企业，我用那家合伙企业的资金购买了伯克希尔。此外，我们两人都没有预料到查理会拥有伯克希尔股票。然而，1965年，查理立即建议我："沃伦，别再想着收购另一家像伯克希尔这样的公司了。但是既然你已经控制了伯克希尔，就用它来增加以公平价格收购的优秀企业，而不是以美妙价格收购一般企业。换句话说，放弃你从你的英雄本·格雷厄姆那里学到的一切。它有效，但只在实践小规模时才有效。"经过多次退步，我随后遵循了他的指示。
English
Many years later, Charlie became my partner in running Berkshire and, repeatedly, jerked me back to sanity when my old habits surfaced. Until his death, he continued in this role and together we, along with those who early on invested with us, ended up far better off than Charlie and I had ever dreamed possible. In reality, Charlie was the "architect" of the present Berkshire, and I acted as the "general contractor" to carry out the day-by-day construction of his vision.
中文翻译
很多年后，查理成为我经营伯克希尔的伙伴，当我旧习惯浮现时，他反复把我拉回理智。直到他去世，他继续扮演这个角色，我们一起，加上那些早期与我们一起投资的人，最终比查理和我曾经梦想的可能要好得多。事实上，查理是现在伯克希尔的"建筑师"，而我充当了"总承包商"，执行他的愿景的日常建设。
English
Charlie never sought to take credit for his role as creator but instead let me take the bows and receive the accolades. In a way his relationship with me was part older brother, part loving father. Even when he knew he was right, he gave me the reins, and when I blundered he never – never – reminded me of my mistake. In the physical world, great buildings are linked to their architect while those who had poured the concrete or installed the windows are soon forgotten. Berkshire has become a great company. Though I have long been in charge of the construction crew; Charlie should forever be credited with being the architect.
中文翻译
查理从未寻求为其创造者角色邀功，而是让我接受掌声和荣誉。在某种程度上，他与我的关系既是兄长，又是慈父。即使他知道自己是正确的，他也把缰绳交给我，而当我犯错时，他从未——从未——提醒过我的错误。在现实世界中，伟大的建筑与其建筑师联系在一起，而那些浇筑混凝土或安装窗户的人很快就会被遗忘。伯克希尔已经成为一家伟大的公司。虽然我长期以来一直负责施工团队；但查理应该永远被归功为建筑师。

致伯克希尔·哈撒韦公司的股东：

English
Berkshire has more than three million shareholder accounts. I am charged with writing a letter every year that will be useful to this diverse and ever-changing group of owners, many of whom wish to learn more about their investment. Charlie Munger, for decades my partner in managing Berkshire, viewed this obligation identically and would expect me to communicate with you this year in the regular manner. He and I were of one mind regarding our responsibilities to Berkshire shareholders.
中文翻译
伯克希尔拥有超过300万个股东账户。我负责每年写一封信，对这个多元化且不断变化的所有者群体有用，其中许多人希望更多地了解他们的投资。几十年来，查理·芒格作为我管理伯克希尔的伙伴，以同样的态度看待这一义务，并期望我今年以常规方式与您沟通。他和我对于对伯克希尔股东的责任有着相同的看法。
English
Writers find it useful to picture the reader they seek, and often they are hoping to attract a mass audience. At Berkshire, we have a more limited target: investors who trust Berkshire with their savings without any expectation of resale (resembling in attitude people who save in order to buy a farm or rental property rather than people who prefer using their excess funds to purchase lottery tickets or "hot" stocks).
中文翻译
作家发现设想他们寻求的读者是有用的，他们经常希望吸引大众观众。在伯克希尔，我们有一个更有限的目标：将储蓄托付给伯克希尔且没有任何转售预期的投资者（态度类似于为购买农场或出租物业而储蓄的人，而不是喜欢用多余资金购买彩票或"热门"股票的人）。
English
Over the years, Berkshire has attracted an unusual number of such "lifetime" shareholders and their heirs. We cherish their presence and believe they are entitled to hear every year both the good and bad news, delivered directly from their CEO and not from an investor-relations officer or communications consultant forever serving up optimism and syrupy mush.
中文翻译
多年来，伯克希尔吸引了异常多的此类"终身"股东及其继承人。我们珍惜他们的存在，并相信他们有权每年听到好消息和坏消息，直接来自他们的CEO，而不是来自永远提供乐观和甜言蜜语的投资者关系官员或传播顾问。
English
In visualizing the owners that Berkshire seeks, I am lucky to have the perfect mental model, my sister, Bertie. Let me introduce her. For openers, Bertie is smart, wise and likes to challenge my thinking. We have never, however, had a shouting match or anything close to a ruptured relationship. We never will. Furthermore, Bertie, and her three daughters as well, have a large portion of their savings in Berkshire shares. Their ownership spans decades, and every year Bertie will read what I have to say. My job is to anticipate her questions and give her honest answers.
中文翻译
在设想伯克希尔寻求的所有者时，我很幸运有一个完美的心理模型，我的妹妹贝蒂。让我介绍她。首先，贝蒂聪明、睿智，喜欢挑战我的想法。然而，我们从来没有过争吵或任何接近破裂关系的事情。我们永远不会。此外，贝蒂和她的三个女儿也将其储蓄的大部分用于伯克希尔股票。她们的所有权跨越了几十年，每年贝蒂都会阅读我要说的话。我的工作是预见她的问题并给她诚实的答案。

运营结果，事实与虚构

English
Let's begin with the numbers. The official annual report begins on K-1 and extends for 124 pages. It is filled with a vast amount of information – some important, some trivial. Among its disclosures many owners, along with financial reporters, will focus on page K-72. There, they will find the proverbial "bottom line" labeled "Net earnings (loss)." The numbers read $90 billion for 2021, ($23 billion) for 2022 and $96 billion for 2023. What in the world is going on?
中文翻译
让我们从数字开始。官方年度报告从K-1开始，延伸124页。它充满了大量信息——有些重要，有些则无关紧要。在其披露中，许多所有者和财经记者将关注K-72页。在那里，他们会找到被称为"底线"的"净收益（亏损）"。数字显示2021年为900亿美元，2022年为（230亿美元），2023年为960亿美元。到底发生了什么？
English
You seek guidance and are told that the procedures for calculating these "earnings" are promulgated by a sober and credentialed Financial Accounting Standards Board ("FASB"), mandated by a dedicated and hard-working Securities and Exchange Commission ("SEC") and audited by the world-class professionals at Deloitte & Touche ("D&T"). So sanctified, this worse-than-useless "net income" figure quickly gets transmitted throughout the world via the internet and media. All parties believe they have done their job – and, legally, they have. We, however, are left uncomfortable.
中文翻译
你寻求指导，被告知这些"收益"的计算程序是由清醒且有资质的财务会计准则委员会（"FASB"）制定的，由勤奋工作的证券交易委员会（"SEC"）授权，并由德勤（"D&T"）的世界级专业人士审计。如此神圣化的这个比无用更糟的"净利润"数字通过互联网和媒体迅速传遍全世界。各方都认为自己完成了工作——而且在法律上，他们确实完成了。然而，我们感到不舒服。
English
At Berkshire, our view is that "earnings" should be a sensible concept that Bertie will find somewhat useful – but only as a starting point – in evaluating a business. Accordingly, Berkshire also reports to Bertie and you what we call "operating earnings." Here is the story they tell: $27.6 billion for 2021; $30.9 billion for 2022 and $37.4 billion for 2023.
中文翻译
在伯克希尔，我们的观点是"收益"应该是一个贝蒂会觉得有些用的合理概念——但只是作为评估企业的起点。因此，伯克希尔也向贝蒂和您报告我们所说的"运营收益"。这是它们讲述的故事：2021年276亿美元；2022年309亿美元；2023年374亿美元。
English
Make no mistake about the significance of capital gains: I expect them to be a very important component of Berkshire's value accretion during the decades ahead. Why else would we commit huge dollar amounts of your money (and Bertie's) to marketable equities just as I have been doing with my own funds throughout my investing lifetime? I can't remember a period since March 11, 1942 – the date of my first stock purchase – that I have not had a majority of my net worth in equities, U.S.-based equities. And so far, so good.
中文翻译
不要误解资本收益的重要性：我期望它们在未来的几十年里成为伯克希尔价值增值的一个非常重要的组成部分。否则，我们为什么要将您的资金（和贝蒂的资金）大量投入可流通股票，就像我整个投资生涯中用我自己的资金所做的那样？自从1942年3月11日——我第一次购买股票的日期——以来，我想不起有哪个时期我的净资产中没有大部分是股票，而且是美国股票。到目前为止，一切顺利。

我们做什么

English
Our goal at Berkshire is simple: We want to own either all or a portion of businesses that enjoy good economics that are fundamental and enduring. Within capitalism, some businesses will flourish for a very long time while others will prove to be sinkholes. It's harder than you would think to predict which will be the winners and losers. And those who tell you they know the answer are usually either self-delusional or snake-oil salesmen.
中文翻译
我们在伯克希尔的目标很简单：我们想要拥有全部或部分具有基本且持久良好经济性的企业。在资本主义体系中，有些企业会长期繁荣，而另一些则会被证明是吸金黑洞。预测哪些将成为赢家和输家比你想象的更难。告诉你他们知道答案的人通常是自欺欺人或者江湖骗子。
English
At Berkshire, we particularly favor the rare enterprise that can deploy additional capital at high returns in the future. Owning only one of these companies – and simply sitting tight – can deliver wealth almost beyond measure. We also hope these favored businesses are run by able and trustworthy managers, though that is a more difficult judgment to make, and Berkshire has had its share of disappointments.
中文翻译
在伯克希尔，我们特别青睐那些未来能够以高回报部署额外资本的罕见企业。仅仅拥有其中一家公司——然后坐着不动——就能带来几乎无法衡量的财富。我们也希望这些受青睐的企业由能干且可信的管理者经营，尽管这是一个更难做出的判断，而且伯克希尔也有过失望的时候。
English
In 1863, Hugh McCulloch, the first Comptroller of the United States, sent a letter to all national banks. His instructions included this warning: "Never deal with a rascal under the expectation that you can prevent him from cheating you." Many bankers who thought they could "manage" the rascal problem have learned the wisdom of Mr. McCulloch's advice – and I have as well. People are not that easy to read. Sincerity and empathy can easily be faked.
中文翻译
1863年，美国第一任审计长休·麦克库洛奇向所有国家银行发出一封信。他的指示包括这个警告："永远不要在与骗子打交道时期望你能阻止他欺骗你。"许多认为他们可以"管理"骗子问题的银行家已经学会了麦克库洛奇先生建议的智慧——我也一样。人们不是那么容易看透的。真诚和同理心很容易被伪装。
English
Those days are long behind us; size did us in, though increased competition for purchases was also a factor. Berkshire now has – by far – the largest GAAP net worth recorded by any American business. Record operating income and a strong stock market led to a yearend figure of $561 billion. By this measure, Berkshire now occupies nearly 6% of the universe in which it operates. Doubling our huge base is simply not possible within, say, a five-year period, particularly because we are highly averse to issuing shares.
中文翻译
那些日子早已远去；规模成了我们的障碍，尽管收购竞争加剧也是一个因素。伯克希尔现在拥有——迄今为止——任何美国企业记录的最大GAAP净资产。创纪录的运营收入和强劲的股票市场导致年底数字达到5610亿美元。以此衡量，伯克希尔现在占其运营领域的近6%。在比如说五年内翻倍我们庞大的基数是不可能的，特别是因为我们非常不愿意发行股票。
English
There remain only a handful of companies in this country capable of truly moving the needle at Berkshire, and they have been endlessly picked over by us and by others. Some we can value; some we can't. And, if we can, they have to be attractively priced. Outside the U.S., there are essentially no candidates that are meaningful options for capital deployment at Berkshire. All in all, we have no possibility of eye-popping performance. Nevertheless, managing Berkshire is mostly fun and always interesting.
中文翻译
在这个国家，只有少数几家公司能够真正影响伯克希尔的业绩，而且它们已经被我们和其他人反复筛选过。有些我们可以估值；有些不能。而且，如果我们能，它们必须价格有吸引力。在美国以外，基本上没有对伯克希尔资本部署有意义的选择。总的来说，我们没有惊人表现的可能性。然而，管理伯克希尔大多很有趣，而且总是有趣的。

我们不太保密的秘密武器

English
Occasionally, markets and/or the economy will cause stocks and bonds of some large and fundamentally good businesses to be strikingly mispriced. Indeed, markets can – and will – unpredictably seize up or even vanish as they did for four months in 1914 and for a few days in 2001. If you believe that American investors are now more stable than in the past, think back to September 2008. Speed of communication and the wonders of technology facilitate instant worldwide paralysis, and we have come a long way since smoke signals.
中文翻译
偶尔，市场和/或经济会导致一些大型且基本面良好的企业股票和债券被显著错误定价。事实上，市场可能——而且将会——不可预测地冻结甚至消失，就像1914年持续了四个月和2001年持续了几天那样。如果你认为美国投资者现在比过去更稳定，回想一下2008年9月。通信速度和技术的奇迹促进了即时的全球瘫痪，自从烟雾信号以来我们已经走过了漫长的道路。
English
Berkshire's ability to immediately respond to market seizures with both huge sums and certainty of performance may offer us an occasional large-scale opportunity. Though the stock market is massively larger than it was in our early years, today's active participants are neither more emotionally stable nor better taught than when I was in school. For whatever reasons, markets now exhibit far more casino-like behavior than they did when I was young. The casino now resides in many homes and daily tempts the occupants.
中文翻译
伯克希尔能够立即以大量资金和确定性回报回应市场冻结，可能为我们提供偶尔的大规模机会。尽管股票市场比早年大了很多，但今天的活跃参与者既不比我在学校时更情绪稳定，也不比那时学得更好。出于某种原因，市场现在表现出比我年轻时更像赌场的行為。赌场现在存在于许多家庭中，每天引诱着居住者。
English
One investment rule at Berkshire has not and will not change: Never risk permanent loss of capital. Thanks to the American tailwind and the power of compound interest, the arena in which we operate has been – and will be – rewarding if you make a couple of good decisions during a lifetime and avoid serious mistakes.
中文翻译
伯克希尔的一条投资规则没有也不会改变：永远不要冒永久损失资本的风险。多亏了美国顺风和复利的力量，我们运营的领域已经——而且将继续——给予奖励，如果你一生中做出几个好的决定并避免严重错误的话。
English
I believe Berkshire can handle financial disasters of a magnitude beyond any heretofore experienced. This ability is one we will not relinquish. When economic upsets occur, as they will, Berkshire's goal will be to function as an asset to the country – just as it was in a very minor way in 2008-9 – and to help extinguish the financial fire rather than to be among the many companies that, inadvertently or otherwise, ignited the conflagration.
中文翻译
我相信伯克希尔能够处理超越任何迄今为止经历的金融灾难。这种能力是我们不会放弃的。当经济动荡发生时——它们会发生——伯克希尔的目标是成为国家的资产——就像2008-09年以非常轻微的方式那样——并帮助扑灭金融火灾，而不是成为那些有意无意地点燃大火的众多公司之一。

让我们感到安心的非控制性企业

English
Last year I mentioned two of Berkshire's long-duration partial-ownership positions – Coca-Cola and American Express. These are not huge commitments like our Apple position. Each only accounts for 4-5% of Berkshire's GAAP net worth. But they are meaningful assets and also illustrate our thought processes. American Express began operations in 1850, and Coca-Cola was launched in an Atlanta drug store in 1886. (Berkshire is not big on newcomers.) Both companies tried expanding into unrelated areas over the years and both found little success in these attempts. But each was hugely successful in its base business, reshaped here and there as conditions called for.
中文翻译
去年我提到了伯克希尔的两个长期部分持股——可口可乐和美国运通。这些不像我们持有的苹果那样是巨额持仓。每家只占伯克希尔GAAP净资产的4-5%。但它们是有意义的资产，也说明了我们的思维过程。美国运通于1850年开始运营，可口可乐于1886年在亚特兰大一家药店推出。（伯克希尔不太看好新进入者。）两家公司多年来都尝试扩展到不相关领域，但都没有在这些尝试中获得多大成功。但每家在其核心业务上都非常成功，并根据条件的变化不时地进行重塑。
English
During 2023, we did not buy or sell a share of either AMEX or Coke – extending our own Rip Van Winkle slumber that has now lasted well over two decades. Both companies again rewarded our inaction last year by increasing their earnings and dividends. Indeed, our share of AMEX earnings in 2023 considerably exceeded the $1.3 billion cost of our long-ago purchase. Could I create a better worldwide business than these two enjoy? As Bertie will tell you: "No way."
中文翻译
在2023年，我们没有买卖美国运通或可口可乐的任何股份——延续了我们已经持续了二十多年的睡美人般的休眠状态。两家公司去年通过增加收益和股息再次奖励了我们的不作为。事实上，我们2023年应得的美国运通收益大大超过了我们很久以前购买时支付的13亿美元成本。我能创造一个比这两家公司所享受的更好的全球业务吗？正如贝蒂会告诉你的："没办法。"
English
The lesson from Coke and AMEX? When you find a truly wonderful business, stick with it. Patience pays, and one wonderful business can offset the many mediocre decisions that are inevitable.
中文翻译
从可口可乐和美国运通学到的教训？当你发现一个真正美妙的企业时，坚持持有它。耐心有回报，一个美妙的企业可以抵消许多不可避免的平庸决策。
English
At yearend, Berkshire owned 27.8% of Occidental Petroleum's common shares and also owned warrants that, for more than five years, give us the option to materially increase our ownership at a fixed price. Though we very much like our ownership, as well as the option, Berkshire has no interest in purchasing or managing Occidental. We particularly like its vast oil and gas holdings in the United States, as well as its leadership in carbon-capture initiatives, though the economic feasibility of this technique has yet to be proven. Both of these activities are very much in our country's interest.
中文翻译
到年底，伯克希尔拥有西方石油公司普通股的27.8%，还拥有认股权证，在五年多以来给我们以固定价格大幅增加持股的期权。虽然我们非常喜欢我们的持股和这个期权，但伯克希尔无意收购或管理西方石油公司。我们特别喜欢其在美国的庞大油气资产，以及在碳捕获倡议方面的领先地位，尽管这项技术的经济可行性尚未得到证实。这两项活动都非常符合我们国家的利益。

奥马哈有什么魔力？
